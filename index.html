Tips

  • Don't let them die
  • Tell them that you love them
  • Keep them in water
  • Don't over feed them
  • Treat them like a family member
  • Make sure to filter the water
  • Understand the personaily of your fish
  • Add coral and rocks into the aquarium
  • Don't mix fresh and saltwater fish
  • Clean the aquarium
  • Give them sunlight
  • Make sure the aquarium is close correcly
  • Don't mix agressive and passive fish
  • Make sure the fish is happy
